Search your music, I'll show you the score and lyrics

With our Meta-Search Engine, you can look up a song in different platforms at the same time and choose the player that you prefer :). Only one lyrics and score engine are supported by now, sorry! It's really simple and straight-forward to use: just query an input text (song title, artist, tag...) and the Streamer Boxes will be full of cool options to listen to. Whether you pick the song form one engine or the other, the main Player window will appear, with a minimal playback control widget, a panel with the retrieved lyrics and the Score of the track. If no score has been found, you'll be prompted to create your own! Please don't use the link that appears there (we know, it's kind of cruel to whou you this nice button...), just click on the tab on the top.
